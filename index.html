FAQ

Our Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a reservation required?

No, reservations are not required, but we recommend making one to ensure a table, especially during peak hours.

Can i host an event at The restaurant?

Yes, you can host private events at our restaurant. Please contact our events manager for details and reservations.

Is valet parking available at the restaurant?

Yes, we offer valet parking services for your convenience.

When are the happy hours of your bar?

Our bar happy hours are from 4 PM to 7 PM, Monday through Friday, offering special drinks and appetizers.

Is seafood available at the restaurant?

Yes, we offer a variety of fresh seafood dishes, which are available daily.
